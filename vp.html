History

15 years ago Violeta Alvarado bought a Wilson cake decorating book and fell in love with cake making and decorating! At the start of her business, she made some business cards and made a cake which she took through her entire neighborhood, delivering slice after slice to anyone who could spare a minute to hear about her newly found business. Today, she is widely considered to be the best baker in all of Splendora, Texas!
